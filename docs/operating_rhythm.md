# PMO Operating Rhythm

## Daily cadence (site stand-up)

Each subproject reports only:

- today's execution focus
- blockers
- whether milestone impact exists

## Weekly cadence

1. Subproject review
- last week completion
- this week plan
- active blockers
- escalation items

2. PMO control review
- all Red/Yellow items
- resource conflicts
- critical procurement
- major risks
- decisions required from leadership
- use `pmo_weekly_digest.md` as the one-page control brief

3. Cost and procurement review
- critical material and delivery
- overrun risk items
- payment arrangement
- supplier issues

## Monthly cadence (operating review)

- bidding / in-delivery / receivable pipeline
- gross margin trend
- subproject lessons and loss points
- next-month key targets
